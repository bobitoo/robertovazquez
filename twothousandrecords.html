twothousand records
a speculative record label that proposes the concept of a creative environment free from commercial influence, encouraging the complete experimentation and exploration of music. TWOTHOUSANDRECORDS comprises of 7 artists all with their own personal brand, music, and websites. all graphical content related to the TWOTHOUSANDRECORDS brand was created using Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects and Premiere Pro. all music was composed in Ableton Live 11 and FL Studio 12. all web design was written using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
